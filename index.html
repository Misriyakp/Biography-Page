Known as "The Wizard of Menlo Park", Edison held over 1,000 patents. His work laid the foundation for the modern electric utility industry.

Despite facing many challenges, including partial deafness, Edison's perseverance and innovative spirit made him one of the greatest inventors in history.

  • Held over 1,093 U.S. Patents
